                               RESOLUTION


 
   Designating Chairman of the Senate Committee on Foreign Relations.

    Resolved, The Senator from New Jersey, Mr. Menendez, shall be the 
Chairman of the Committee on Foreign Relations for the One Hundred 
Thirteenth Congress, or until his successor is chosen.
    Sec. 2.  Provided, That this resolution shall be effective upon the 
resignation of the Senator from Massachusetts (Mr. Kerry).
