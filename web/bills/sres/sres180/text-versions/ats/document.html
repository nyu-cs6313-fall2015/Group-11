                               RESOLUTION


 
       Making minority party appointments for the 113th Congress.

    Resolved, That the following be the minority membership on the 
following committees for the remainder of the 113th Congress, or until 
their successors are appointed:
    Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation: Mr. Thune, Mr. 
Wicker, Mr. Blunt, Mr. Rubio, Ms. Ayotte, Mr. Heller, Mr. Coats, Mr. 
Scott, Mr. Cruz, Mrs. Fischer, Mr. Johnson of Wisconsin, and Mr. 
Chiesa.
    Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs: Mr. 
Coburn, Mr. McCain, Mr. Johnson, Mr. Portman, Mr. Paul, Mr. Enzi, Ms. 
Ayotte, and Mr. Chiesa.
    Committee on Small Business and Entrepreneurship: Mr. Risch, Mr. 
Vitter, Mr. Rubio, Mr. Paul, Mr. Scott, Mrs. Fischer, Mr. Enzi, Mr. 
Johnson of Wisconsin, and Mr. Chiesa.
