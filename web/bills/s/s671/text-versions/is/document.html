                                 A BILL


 
                  For the relief of Deniss Nikanorov.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. NATURALIZATION FOR DENISS NIKANOROV.

    Notwithstanding title III of the Immigration and Nationality Act (8 
U.S.C. 1401 et seq.), or any other provision of law relating to 
naturalization, Deniss Nikanorov is deemed to be a naturalized citizen 
of the United States as of the date of the enactment of this Act.
