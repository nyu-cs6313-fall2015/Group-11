                                 A BILL


 
To designate the community based outpatient clinic of the Department of 
   Veterans Affairs located in The Dalles, Oregon, as the ``Loren R. 
                  Kaufman Memorial Veterans' Clinic''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. LOREN R. KAUFMAN MEMORIAL VETERANS' CLINIC.

    (a) Designation.--The community based outpatient clinic of the 
Department of Veterans Affairs located at 704 Veterans Drive, The 
Dalles, Oregon, shall after the date of the enactment of this Act be 
known and designated as the ``Loren R. Kaufman Memorial Veterans' 
Clinic''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in any law, regulation, map, 
document, record, or other paper of the United States to the community 
based outpatient clinic referred to in subsection (a) shall be 
considered to be a reference to the ``Loren R. Kaufman Memorial 
Veterans' Clinic''.
