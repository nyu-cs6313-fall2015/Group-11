                                 A BILL


 
 To improve the transition between experimental permits and commercial 
           licenses for commercial reusable launch vehicles.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. COMMERCIAL SPACE LAUNCH LICENSING.

    Section 50906 of title 51, United States Code, is amended--
            (1) in subsection (e)(1), by striking ``suborbital rocket 
        design'' and inserting ``suborbital rocket or rocket design''; 
        and
            (2) by amending subsection (g) to read as follows:
    ``(g) A permit may be issued, and a permit that has already been 
issued shall remain valid for the uses described in subsection (d), for 
a particular reusable suborbital rocket or rocket design after a 
license has been issued for the launch or reentry of a rocket of that 
design.''.
