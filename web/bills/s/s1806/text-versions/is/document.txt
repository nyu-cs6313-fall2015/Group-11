                                 A BILL


 
  To amend the Federal Home Loan Bank Act with respect to membership 
                  eligibility of certain institutions.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. INCLUSION OF CERTAIN DEPOSITORY INSTITUTIONS IN FEDERAL HOME 
              LOAN BANK SYSTEM.

    Section 4(a)(1) of the Federal Home Loan Bank Act (12 U.S.C. 
1424(a)(1)) is amended by striking ``(as defined in section 2 of this 
Act)'' and inserting ``(as defined in section 43(e)(2) of the Federal 
Deposit Insurance Act (12 U.S.C. 1831t(e)(2)))''.
