                                 A BILL


 
  To designate the Federal building located at 118 South Mill Street, 
   Fergus Falls, Minnesota, as the ``Edward J. Devitt United States 
                             Courthouse''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EDWARD J. DEVITT UNITED STATES COURTHOUSE.

    (a) Designation.--The Federal building located at 118 South Mill 
Street, Fergus Falls, Minnesota, shall be known and designated as the 
``Edward J. Devitt United States Courthouse''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the Federal building 
referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the 
``Edward J. Devitt United States Courthouse''.
