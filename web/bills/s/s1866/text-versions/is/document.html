                                 A BILL


 
 To provide for an extension of the legislative authority of the Adams 
   Memorial Foundation to establish a commemorative work in honor of 
              former President John Adams and his legacy.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EXTENSION OF LEGISLATIVE AUTHORITY FOR MEMORIAL 
              ESTABLISHMENT.

    Section 1(c) of Public Law 107-62, as amended by Public Law 111-
169, is amended by striking ``2013'' and inserting ``2020''.
