                                 A BILL


 
      To repeal section 403 of the Bipartisan Budget Act of 2013.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REPEAL OF CERTAIN REDUCTIONS MADE BY THE BIPARTISAN BUDGET 
              ACT OF 2013.

    Section 403 of the Bipartisan Budget Act of 2013 (Public Law 113-
67) is repealed as of the date of the enactment of such Act.
