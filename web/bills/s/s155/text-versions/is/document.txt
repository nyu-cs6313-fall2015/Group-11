                                 A BILL


 
       To designate a mountain in the State of Alaska as Denali.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION OF DENALI IN THE STATE OF ALASKA.

    (a) In General.--The mountain located at 63 04' 12" N, by 151 00' 
18" W, in the State of Alaska shall be known and designated as 
``Denali''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the mountain described 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to ``Denali''.
