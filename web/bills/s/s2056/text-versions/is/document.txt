                                 A BILL


 
 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located 
at 13127 Broadway Street in Alden, New York, as the ``Sergeant Brett E. 
                   Gornewicz Memorial Post Office''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SERGEANT BRETT E. GORNEWICZ MEMORIAL POST OFFICE.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 13127 Broadway Street in Alden, New York, shall be known and 
designated as the ``Sergeant Brett E. Gornewicz Memorial Post Office''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Sergeant 
Brett E. Gornewicz Memorial Post Office''.
