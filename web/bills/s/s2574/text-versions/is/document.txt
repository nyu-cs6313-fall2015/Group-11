                                 A BILL


 
To make the United States Preventive Services Task Force subject to the 
                    Federal Advisory Committee Act.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Healthy Families Act of 2014''.

SEC. 2. UNITED STATES PREVENTIVE SERVICES TASK FORCE.

    Section 915(a) of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 299b-
4(a)) is amended by striking paragraph (5) and inserting the following:
            ``(5) Applicability of faca.--The Federal Advisory 
        Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App.) shall apply to the Task Force.''.
