                                 A BILL


 
 To reauthorize the Reclamation States Emergency Drought Relief Act of 
                     1991, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EXTENSION OF RECLAMATION STATES EMERGENCY DROUGHT RELIEF ACT 
              OF 1991.

    (a) Termination of Authority.--Section 104(c) of the Reclamation 
States Emergency Drought Relief Act of 1991 (43 U.S.C. 2214(c)) is 
amended by striking ``2012'' and inserting ``2018''.
    (b) Authorization of Appropriations.--Section 301 of the 
Reclamation States Emergency Drought Relief Act of 1991 (43 U.S.C. 
2241) is amended--
            (1) by striking ``$90,000,000'' and inserting 
        ``$110,000,000''; and
            (2) by striking ``2012'' and inserting ``2018''.
