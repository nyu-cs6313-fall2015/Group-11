                                 A BILL


 
              To reauthorize the Congressional Award Act.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Congressional Award Program 
Reauthorization Act of 2013''.

SEC. 2. TERMINATION.

    Section 108 of the Congressional Award Act (2 U.S.C. 808) is 
amended by striking ``October 1, 2013'' and inserting ``October 1, 
2018''.
