                                 A BILL


 
    To amend the Public Health Service Act to include occupational 
 therapists as behavioral and mental health professionals for purposes 
                 of the National Health Service Corps.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Occupational Therapy in Mental 
Health Act''.

SEC. 2. INCLUDING OCCUPATIONAL THERAPISTS AS BEHAVIORAL AND MENTAL 
              HEALTH PROFESSIONALS FOR PURPOSES OF THE NATIONAL HEALTH 
              SERVICE CORPS.

    Section 331(a)(1)(E)(i) of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 
254d(a)(1)(E)(i)) is amended by inserting ``occupational therapists,'' 
after ``psychiatric nurse specialists,''.
