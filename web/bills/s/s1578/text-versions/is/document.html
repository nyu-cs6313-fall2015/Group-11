                                 A BILL


 
   To authorize the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to cover the costs 
     associated with the care of veterans at medical foster homes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. COVERAGE OF COSTS OF CARE FOR VETERANS AT MEDICAL FOSTER 
              HOMES.

    In conducting the medical foster home program pursuant to section 
17.73 of title 38, Code of Federal Regulations, the Secretary of 
Veterans Affairs may cover the costs associated with the care of 
veterans at medical foster homes.
