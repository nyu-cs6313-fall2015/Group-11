                                 A BILL


 
 To prohibit the payment of performance awards in fiscal year 2015 to 
    employees in the Veterans Health Administration, and for other 
                               purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PROHIBITION ON PAYMENT OF PERFORMANCE AWARDS TO EMPLOYEES IN 
              VETERANS HEALTH ADMINISTRATION.

    Notwithstanding section 5384 of title 5, United States Code, in 
fiscal year 2015, no performance award shall be paid under such section 
to employees in the Veterans Health Administration.
