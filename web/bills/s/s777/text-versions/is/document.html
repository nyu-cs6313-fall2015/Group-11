                                 A BILL


 
    To restore the previous policy regarding restrictions on use of 
               Department of Defense medical facilities.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. RESTORATION OF PREVIOUS POLICY REGARDING RESTRICTIONS ON USE 
              OF DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE MEDICAL FACILITIES.

    Section 1093 of title 10, United States Code, is amended--
            (1) by striking subsection (b); and
            (2) in subsection (a), by striking ``(a) Restriction on Use 
        of Funds.--''.
