                               RESOLUTION


 
   Providing for consideration of the bill (H.R. 4719) to amend the 
  Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to permanently extend and expand the 
       charitable deduction for contributions of food inventory.

    Resolved, That upon adoption of this resolution it shall be in 
order to consider in the House the bill (H.R. 4719) to amend the 
Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to permanently extend and expand the 
charitable deduction for contributions of food inventory. All points of 
order against consideration of the bill are waived. In lieu of the 
amendment in the nature of a substitute recommended by the Committee on 
Ways and Means now printed in the bill, an amendment in the nature of a 
substitute consisting of the text of Rules Committee Print 113-51 shall 
be considered as adopted. The bill, as amended, shall be considered as 
read. All points of order against provisions in the bill, as amended, 
are waived. The previous question shall be considered as ordered on the 
bill, as amended, and on any further amendment thereto, to final 
passage without intervening motion except: (1) one hour of debate 
equally divided and controlled by the chair and ranking minority member 
of the Committee on Ways and Means; and (2) one motion to recommit with 
or without instructions.
