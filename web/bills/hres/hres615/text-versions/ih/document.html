                               RESOLUTION


 
 Expressing the sense of the House of Representatives that Members who 
vote in favor of the establishment of a public, Federal Government run 
 health insurance option are urged to forgo their right to participate 
 in the Federal Employees Health Benefits Program (FEHBP) and agree to 
                    enroll under that public option.

    Resolved, That it is the sense of the House of Representatives that 
Members who vote in favor of the establishment of a public, Federal 
Government run health insurance option are urged to forgo their right 
to participate in the Federal Employees Health Benefits Program (FEHBP) 
and agree to enroll under that public option.
