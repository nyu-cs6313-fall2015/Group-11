H. Res. 379

                In the House of Representatives, U. S.,

                                                      October 11, 2013.
    Resolved, That it is the sense of the House of Representatives that the 
managers on the part of the House of the conference on the disagreeing votes of 
the two Houses on the House amendment to the Senate amendment to the bill H.R. 
2642 (an Act to provide for the reform and continuation of agricultural and 
other programs of the Department of Agriculture and other programs of the 
Department of Agriculture through fiscal year 2018, and for other purposes) 
should--
            (1) agree to provisions relating to a limitation on premium subsidy 
        based on average adjusted gross income in excess of $750,000;
            (2) agree to provisions relating to a requirement for the Secretary 
        to carry out a study on crop insurance and the impacts of an adjusted 
        gross income limitation, as specified in paragraph (1); and
            (3) not agree to provisions relating to a delayed effective date.
            Attest:

                                                                          Clerk.
