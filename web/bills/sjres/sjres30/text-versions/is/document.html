                            JOINT RESOLUTION


 
To disapprove a rule of the Environmental Protection Agency relating to 
    greenhouse gas emissions from electric utility generating units.

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That Congress disapproves the 
rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to new 
source performance standards for emissions of carbon dioxide for new 
affected fossil fuel-fired electric utility generating units (published 
at 79 Fed. Reg. 1430 (January 8, 2014)), and such rule shall have no 
force or effect.
