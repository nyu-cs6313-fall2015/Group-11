                                 A BILL


 
To prohibit officers and employees of the Internal Revenue Service from 
                initiating any new audits for 180 days.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``HALT the IRS Act''.

SEC. 2. SUSPENSION OF AUTHORITY OF IRS TO INITIATE NEW AUDITS.

    An officer or employee of the Internal Revenue Service shall not 
initiate any new audit of any taxpayer during the 180-day period 
beginning with the date of the enactment of this Act.
