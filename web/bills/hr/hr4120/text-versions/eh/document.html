                                 AN ACT


 
    To amend the National Law Enforcement Museum Act to extend the 
                           termination date.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. NATIONAL LAW ENFORCEMENT MUSEUM ACT TERMINATION DATE 
              EXTENDED.

    Section 4(f) of the National Law Enforcement Museum Act (Public Law 
106-492) is amended by striking ``13 years'' and inserting ``16 
years''.

SEC. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE.

    The provisions of this Act shall take effect as if this Act were 
enacted on November 8, 2013.

            Passed the House of Representatives April 28, 2014.
