                                 A BILL


 
   To transfer criminal enforcement and investigative authority and 
 functions of certain agencies to the Federal Bureau of Investigation.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. TRANSFER OF CRIMINAL ENFORCEMENT AND INVESTIGATIVE AUTHORITY 
              AND FUNCTIONS TO THE FEDERAL BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION.

    (a) Repeal of EPA Powers.--Section 3063 of title 18, United States 
Code, is repealed.
    (b) Transfer of Authority to FBI.--Notwithstanding any other 
provision of law, any criminal enforcement authority and functions and 
any criminal investigative authority and functions of the Environmental 
Protection Agency shall be transferred to the Director of the Federal 
Bureau of Investigation, except for any criminal enforcement authority 
and functions and any criminal investigative authority and functions 
delegated to State or local law enforcement.
