                                 A BILL


 
 To amend the Homeland Security Act of 2002 to authorize use of grants 
    under the Urban Area Security Initiative and the State Homeland 
  Security Grant Program to work in conjunction with a Department of 
                      Energy national laboratory.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``National Laboratories Mean National 
Security Act''.

SEC. 2. USE OF HOMELAND SECURITY GRANT FUNDS IN CONJUNCTION WITH 
              DEPARTMENT OF ENERGY NATIONAL LABORATORIES.

    Section 2008(a) of the Homeland Security Act of 2002 (6 U.S.C. 
609(a)) is amended in the matter preceding paragraph (1) by inserting 
``including by working in conjunction with a National Laboratory (as 
defined in section 2(3) of the Energy Policy Act of 2005 (42 U.S.C. 
15801(3)),'' after ``plans,''.
