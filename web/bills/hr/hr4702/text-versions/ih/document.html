                                 A BILL


 
  To extend until 2030 the time period required to be covered by the 
                United States Space Protection Strategy.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Space Protection Strategy Extension 
Act''.

SEC. 2. EXTENSION OF TIME PERIOD TO BE COVERED BY THE SPACE PROTECTION 
              STRATEGY.

    Section 911(d) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal 
Year 2008 (10 U.S.C. 2271 note) is amended by adding at the end the 
following new paragraph:
            ``(4) Fiscal years 2026 through 2030.''.
