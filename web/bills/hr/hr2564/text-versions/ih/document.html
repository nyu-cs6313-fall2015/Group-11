                                 A BILL


 
               To extend the additional duty on ethanol.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EXTENSION OF ADDITIONAL DUTY ON ETHANOL.

    (a) In General.--Heading 9901.00.50 of the Harmonized Tariff 
Schedule of the United States is amended by striking ``1/1/2012'' in 
the effective period column and inserting ``1/1/2016''.
    (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by subsection (a) applies 
to goods entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or 
after the 15th day after the date of the enactment of this Act.
