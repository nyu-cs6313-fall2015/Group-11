                                 AN ACT


 
 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located 
at 103 Center Street West in Eatonville, Washington, as the ``National 
              Park Ranger Margaret Anderson Post Office''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. NATIONAL PARK RANGER MARGARET ANDERSON POST OFFICE.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 103 Center Street West in Eatonville, Washington, shall be 
known and designated as the ``National Park Ranger Margaret Anderson 
Post Office''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``National 
Park Ranger Margaret Anderson Post Office''.

            Passed the House of Representatives March 24, 2014.
