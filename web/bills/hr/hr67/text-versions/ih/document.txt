                                 A BILL


 
To amend subtitle IV of title 40, United States Code, regarding county 
                  additions to the Appalachian region.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. ADDITIONS TO APPALACHIAN REGION.

    Section 14102(a)(1)(K) of title 40, United States Code, is 
amended--
            (1) by inserting ``Hickman,'' after ``Hawkins,'';
            (2) by inserting ``Perry,'' after ``Overton,''; and
            (3) by inserting ``Wayne,'' after ``Washington,''.
