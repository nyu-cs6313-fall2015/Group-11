                                 A BILL


 
 To amend the Oil Pollution Act of 1990 to require responsible parties 
  to pay the full cost of offshore oil spills, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Big Oil Bailout Prevention Act of 
2013''.

SEC. 2. REMOVAL OF LIMITS ON LIABILITY FOR OFFSHORE FACILITIES.

    (a) In General.--Section 1004(a) of the Oil Pollution Act of 1990 
(33 U.S.C. 2704(a)) is amended--
            (1) in paragraph (2), by inserting ``and'' after the 
        semicolon;
            (2) by striking paragraph (3); and
            (3) by redesignating paragraph (4) as paragraph (3).
    (b) Conforming Amendments.--Section 1004(b)(2) of the Oil Pollution 
Act of 1990 (33 U.S.C. 2704(b)(2)) is amended by striking the second 
sentence.
