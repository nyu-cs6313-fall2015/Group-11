                                 A BILL


 
    To reauthorize the America's Agricultural Heritage Partnership.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``America's Agricultural Heritage 
Partnership Reauthorization Act''.

SEC. 2. REAUTHORIZATION OF THE AMERICA'S AGRICULTURAL HERITAGE 
              PARTNERSHIP.

    Section 707 of division II of the Omnibus Parks and Public Lands 
Management Act of 1996 (16 U.S.C. 461 note; Public Law 104-333) is 
amended by striking ``September 30, 2013'' and inserting ``September 
30, 2023''.
