                                 A BILL


 
 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to repeal the increase in 
  the income threshold used in determining the deduction for medical 
                                 care.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Helping Families and Seniors Save on 
Health Care Act of 2013''.

SEC. 2. REPEAL OF INCREASE IN INCOME THRESHOLD FOR DETERMINING MEDICAL 
              CARE DEDUCTION.

    Section 9013 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (and 
the amendments made by such section) are hereby repealed, and the 
Internal Revenue Code of 1986 shall be applied and administered as if 
such section (and amendments) had not been enacted.
