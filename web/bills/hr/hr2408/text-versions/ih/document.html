                                 A BILL


 
To prohibit the Department of Justice from tracking and cataloguing the 
               purchases of multiple rifles and shotguns.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PROHIBITION OF TRACKING OR CATALOGUING FIREARMS PURCHASES.

    For fiscal year 2013 and each fiscal year thereafter, no Federal 
funds may be used to require or continue to require a person licensed 
under section 923 of title 18, United States Code, to report 
information to the Department of Justice regarding the sale of multiple 
rifles or shotguns to the same person.
