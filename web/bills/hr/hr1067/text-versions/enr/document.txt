                                 An Act


 
To make revisions in title 36, United States Code, as necessary to keep 
   the title current and make technical corrections and improvements.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. TABLE OF CONTENTS.

  The table of contents for this Act is as follows:

Sec. 1.  Table of contents.
Sec. 2.  Purpose.
Sec. 3.  Technical amendments.

SEC. 2. PURPOSE.

  The purpose of this Act is to make revisions in title 36, United 
States Code, as necessary to keep the title current and make technical 
corrections and improvements.
