                                 A BILL


 
 To require a 50 percent reduction in the number of limousines in the 
                             Federal fleet.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REDUCTION IN LIMOUSINES IN FEDERAL FLEET.

    (a) Requirement.--Notwithstanding any other provision of law and 
except as provided in subsection (b), for fiscal year 2015 and each 
fiscal year thereafter, the number of limousines in the Federal fleet 
shall not exceed 50 percent of the number of limousines in the Federal 
fleet as of the last day of fiscal year 2014.
    (b) Exception for National Security.--Subsection (a) shall not 
apply with respect to any limousine determined by the President to be 
essential for reasons of national security.
