                                 A BILL


 
  To prohibit the United States Department of Homeland Security from 
 purchasing, operating, or maintaining armed unmanned aerial vehicles.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``No Armed Drones for DHS Act''.

SEC. 2. PROHIBITION ON PURCHASE, USE, OR MAINTENANCE OF ARMED DRONES.

    The United States Department of Homeland Security is prohibited 
from purchasing, operating, or maintaining armed unmanned aerial 
vehicles.
