                                 A BILL


 
To reauthorize the Integrated Coastal and Ocean Observation System Act 
                                of 2009.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REAUTHORIZATION OF INTEGRATED COASTAL AND OCEAN OBSERVATION 
              SYSTEM ACT OF 2009.

    The Integrated Coastal and Ocean Observation System Act of 2009 is 
amended by striking ``are authorized to be appropriated to the 
Secretary of Commerce for fiscal years 2009 through 2013 such sums as 
are necessary'' and inserting ``is authorized to be appropriated to the 
Secretary of Commerce for each of fiscal years 2014 through 2018 
$29,600,000''.
