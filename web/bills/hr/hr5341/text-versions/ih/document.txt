                                 A BILL


 
  To delay for 1 year the application of Revenue Ruling 2012-18 with 
respect to the characterization of payments as tips or service charges.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DELAY IN APPLICATION OF REVENUE RULING 2012-18 WITH RESPECT 
              TO CHARACTERIZATION OF TIPS OR SERVICE CHARGES.

    The Secretary of the Treasury shall not apply Revenue Ruling 2012-
18 (or any other similar guidance) for purposes of determining whether 
any payment made before January 1, 2015, is a tip as opposed to a 
service charge.
