                                 A BILL


 
To recognize a primary measure of national unemployment for purposes of 
                        the Federal Government.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Real Unemployment Calculation Act''.

SEC. 2. PRIMARY MEASURE OF UNEMPLOYMENT.

    The measure of unemployment issued by the Bureau of Labor 
Statistics known as U5 shall be treated by the Bureau as the official 
unemployment rate and shall be considered by the Federal Government as 
the primary measure of unemployment in the United States.
