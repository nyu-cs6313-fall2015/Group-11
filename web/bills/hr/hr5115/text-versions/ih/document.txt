                                 A BILL


 
   To amend title 38, United States Code, to improve the beneficiary 
         travel program of the Department of Veterans Affairs.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Rural Veteran Access to Care Act''.

SEC. 2. EXPANSION OF BENEFICIARY TRAVEL PROGRAM.

    Section 111(b)(1)(B) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by 
striking ``rated at 30 percent or more''.
