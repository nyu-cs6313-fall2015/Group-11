                                 A BILL


 
  To prohibit an increase in the number of flag and general officers.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. NO INCREASE IN FLAG OR GENERAL OFFICERS.

    No funds appropriated for fiscal year 2014 may be used for flag or 
general officers for each military department that are in excess to the 
number of such officers serving in such military department as of the 
date of enactment of this Act.
