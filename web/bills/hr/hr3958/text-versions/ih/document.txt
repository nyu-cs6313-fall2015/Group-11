                                 A BILL


 
   To provide for a one-year extension of the Department of Veterans 
  Affairs pilot program on assisted living services for veterans with 
                        traumatic brain injury.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Healing Our Heroes Act''.

SEC. 2. ONE-YEAR EXTENSION OF DEPARTMENT OF VETERANS AFFAIRS PILOT 
              PROGRAM ON ASSISTED LIVING SERVICES FOR VETERANS WITH 
              TRAUMATIC BRAIN INJURY.

    Section 1705 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal 
Year 2008 (Public Law 110-181; 122 Stat. 491; 38 U.S.C. 1710C note) is 
amended--
            (1) in subsection (a), by striking ``five-year pilot 
        program'' and inserting ``six-year pilot program''; and
            (2) in subsection (e)(1), by striking ``the completion of 
        the pilot program'' and inserting ``the date on which the pilot 
        program has been carried out for five years''.
