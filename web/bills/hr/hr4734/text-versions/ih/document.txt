                                 A BILL


 
To repeal the authority of the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection 
             to restrict mandatory pre-dispute arbitration.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Bureau Arbitration Fairness Act''.

SEC. 2. REPEAL OF AUTHORITY TO RESTRICT MANDATORY PRE-DISPUTE 
              ARBITRATION.

    Section 1028 of the Consumer Financial Protection Act of 2010 (12 
U.S.C. 5518) is amended--
            (1) by striking ``(a) Study and Report.--''; and
            (2) by striking subsections (b), (c), and (d).
