                                 A BILL


 
To amend the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008 to repeal the authority to 
            make performance-based bonus payments to States.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Stopping Needless Additional 
Performance Bonuses (SNAP) Act of 2013''.

SEC. 2. AMENDMENTS.

    (a) Repealer.--Section 16 of the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008 (7 
U.S.C. 2025) is amended by striking subsection (d).
    (b) Conforming Amendments.--Section 16(c) of the Food and Nutrition 
Act of 2008 (7 U.S.C. 2025(c)) is amended--
            (1) in the first sentence of paragraph (4) by striking ``, 
        or performance under the performance measures under subsection 
        (d)'', and
            (2) in the first sentence of paragraph (5) by striking ``, 
        or performance under the performance measures under subsection 
        (d)''.
