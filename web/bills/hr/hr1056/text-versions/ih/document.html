                                 A BILL


 
     To extend the temporary suspension of duty on Acid Black 132.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. ACID BLACK 132.

    (a) In General.--Heading 9902.03.59 of the Harmonized Tariff 
Schedule of the United States (relating to Acid Black 132) is amended 
by striking the date in the effective period column and inserting ``12/
31/2016''.
    (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by subsection (a) applies 
to goods entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or 
after the 15th day after the date of the enactment of this Act.
