                                 A BILL


 
 To amend the Small Business Jobs Act of 2010 to extend and expand the 
         State Trade and Export Promotion (STEP) Grant Program.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Small Business Growth Through 
Exports Act of 2013''.

SEC. 2. EXTENSION AND EXPANSION OF STEP GRANT PROGRAM.

    Section 1207 of the Small Business Jobs Act of 2010 (15 U.S.C. 649b 
note) is amended--
            (1) in subsection (i), by striking ``2013'' and inserting 
        ``2013, and $60,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2014, 2015, 
        and 2016''; and
            (2) in subsection (j), by striking ``3'' and inserting 
        ``6''.
