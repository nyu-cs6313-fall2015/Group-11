                                 A BILL


 
   To amend title 39, United States Code, to allow the United States 
 Postal Service to provide nonpostal services, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Expand Nonpostal Services to 
Americans Act''.

SEC. 2. AUTHORITY TO OFFER NONPOSTAL SERVICES.

    Section 404 of title 39, United States Code, is amended--
            (1) in subsection (a)--
                    (A) in paragraph (7), by striking ``and'' at the 
                end;
                    (B) in paragraph (8), by striking the period and 
                inserting ``; and''; and
                    (C) by adding at the end the following:
            ``(9) to provide nonpostal services.''; and
            (2) by striking subsection (e).
