                                 A BILL


 
To amend the National Historic Preservation Act to direct the Secretary 
    of the Interior to provide technical or financial assistance to 
    Hispanic-serving institutions for the establishment of historic 
               preservation training and degree programs.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Preservation Research at 
Institutions Serving Minorities Act'' or ``PRISM Act''.

SEC. 2. HISTORIC PRESERVATION TRAINING AND DEGREE PROGRAMS FOR 
              HISPANIC-SERVING INSTITUTIONS.

    Subparagraph (C) of section 101(j)(2) of the National Historic 
Preservation Act (16 U.S.C. 470a(j)(2)) is amended--
            (1) by striking ``and'' after ``tribal colleges,''; and
            (2) by inserting after ``Hawaiians,'' the following: ``and 
        to Hispanic-serving institutions as defined in section 
        502(a)(5) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 (20 U.S.C. 
        1101a(a)(5)),''.
