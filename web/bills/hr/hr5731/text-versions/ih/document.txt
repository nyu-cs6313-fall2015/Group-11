                                 A BILL


 
To extend foreclosure and eviction protections for servicemembers, and 
                          for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Servicemembers Foreclosure 
Protection Act of 2014''.

SEC. 2. EXTENSION OF PROTECTIONS FOR MEMBERS OF UNIFORMED SERVICES 
              RELATING TO MORTGAGES, MORTGAGE FORECLOSURE, AND 
              EVICTION.

    (a) In General.--Paragraph (1) of section 710(d) of the Honoring 
America's Veterans and Caring for Camp Lejeune Families Act of 2012 
(Public Law 112-154; 126 Stat. 1208) is amended by striking ``2014'' 
and inserting ``2016''.
    (b) Repeal of Revival Provision.--Section 710(d) of the Honoring 
America's Veterans and Caring for Camp Lejeune Families Act of 2012 
(Public Law 112-154; 126 Stat. 1208) is amended by striking paragraph 
(3).
