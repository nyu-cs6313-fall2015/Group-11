                                 A BILL


 
  To amend the Federal charter of the Veterans of Foreign Wars of the 
 United States to reflect the service of women in the Armed Forces of 
                           the United States.


 


    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REFLECTION OF SERVICE OF WOMEN IN THE ARMED FORCES IN THE 
              FEDERAL CHARTER OF THE VETERANS OF FOREIGN WARS OF THE 
              UNITED STATES.

    (a) Organization.--Section 230101(a) of title 36, United States 
Code, is amended by striking ``men'' and inserting ``veterans''.
    (b) Purposes.--Section 230102(3) of such title is amended by 
striking ``widows'' and inserting ``surviving spouses''.
