                                 A BILL


 
       To tax capital gains at the same rate as ordinary income.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Dollar is a Dollar Act''.

SEC. 2. REPEAL OF CAPITAL GAINS TAX CAP.

    Subsection (h), entitled ``Maximum capital gains rate'', of section 
1 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is hereby repealed.
