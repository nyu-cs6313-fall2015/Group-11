                                 A BILL


 
    To include the county of Mohave, in the State of Arizona, as an 
    affected area for purposes of making claims under the Radiation 
  Exposure Compensation Act based on exposure to atmospheric nuclear 
                                testing.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Mohave County Radiation Compensation 
Act of 2013''.

SEC. 2. INCLUSION UNDER THE RADIATION EXPOSURE COMPENSATION ACT.

    Section 4(b)(1)(C) of the Radiation Exposure Compensation Act (42 
U.S.C. 2210 note) is amended by inserting ``Mohave,'' after 
``Apache,''.
