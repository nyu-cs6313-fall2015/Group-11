                         CONCURRENT RESOLUTION

    Resolved by the House of Representatives (the Senate concurring),

SECTION 1. USE OF ROTUNDA FOR CEREMONY TO AWARD CONGRESSIONAL GOLD 
              MEDAL TO PROFESSOR MUHAMMAD YUNUS.

    The rotunda of the Capitol is authorized to be used on April 17, 
2013, for a ceremony to award the 


              

Congressional Gold Medal to Professor Muhammad Yunus in recognition of 
his contributions to the fight against global poverty. Physical 
preparations for the ceremony shall be carried out in accordance with 
such conditions as the Architect of the Capitol may prescribe.

            Passed the House of Representatives March 6, 2013.
