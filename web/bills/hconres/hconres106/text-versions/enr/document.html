                          Concurrent Resolution

    Resolved by the House of Representatives (the Senate concurring),

SECTION 1. USE OF EMANCIPATION HALL FOR GOLD MEDAL CEREMONY IN HONOR OF 
              FALLEN HEROES OF 9/11.

    Emancipation Hall in the Capitol Visitor Center is authorized to be 
used on September 10, 2014, for a ceremony to award Congressional Gold 
Medals in honor of the men and women who perished as a result of the 
terrorist attacks on the United States on September 11, 2001. Physical 
preparations for the conduct of the ceremony shall be carried out in 
accordance with such conditions as may be prescribed by the Architect 
of the Capitol.
