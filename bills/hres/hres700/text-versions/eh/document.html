H. Res. 700

                In the House of Representatives, U. S.,

                                                        August 1, 2014.
    Resolved, That the requirement of clause 6(a) of rule XIII for a two-thirds 
vote to consider a report from the Committee on Rules on the same day it is 
presented to the House is waived with respect to any resolution reported through 
the legislative day of September 5, 2014, providing for consideration or 
disposition of measures relating to the ongoing humanitarian crisis on the U.S. 
southern border, border security, and related immigration law.
    Sec. 2.  It shall be in order at any time through the legislative day of 
September 5, 2014, for the Speaker to entertain motions that the House suspend 
the rules as though under clause 1 of rule XV, relating to measures addressing 
the ongoing humanitarian crisis on the U.S. southern border, border security, 
and related immigration law.
