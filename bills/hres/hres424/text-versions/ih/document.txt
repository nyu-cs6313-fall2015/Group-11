                    IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

                           November 20, 2013

Ms. Slaughter submitted the following resolution; which was referred to 
                         the Committee on Rules

_______________________________________________________________________

                               RESOLUTION


 
Prohibiting the consideration of a concurrent resolution providing for 
  adjournment unless the House has adopted a conference report on the 
                           budget resolution.

    Resolved, That it shall not be in order to consider a concurrent 
resolution providing for adjournment unless the House has adopted a 
conference report on S. Con. Res. 8, establishing a budget for the 
United States Government, by December 13, 2013.
