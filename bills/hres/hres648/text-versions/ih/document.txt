                    IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

                             June 25, 2014

Mr. Stockman submitted the following resolution; which was referred to 
                 the Committee on House Administration

_______________________________________________________________________

                               RESOLUTION


 
 Directing the Chairman of the Federal Election Commission to transmit 
to the House of Representatives copies of any emails in the possession 
of the Commission that were transmitted to or from the email account(s) 
   of former Internal Revenue Service Exempt Organizations Division 
       Director Lois Lerner between January 2009 and April 2011.

    Resolved, That the House of Representatives direct the Chairman of 
the Federal Election Commission to transmit to the House of 
Representatives, no later than 14 days after the date of the adoption 
of this resolution, copies of any electronic communication in the 
possession of the Chairman or the Federal Election Commission that was 
transmitted to or from any electronic mail account(s) used by former 
Internal Revenue Service Exempt Organizations Division Director Lois 
Lerner at any time between January 1, 2009, and April 30, 2011.
