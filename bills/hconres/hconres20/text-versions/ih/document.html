                         CONCURRENT RESOLUTION


 
  Permitting the use of the rotunda of the Capitol for a ceremony to 
     award the Congressional Gold Medal to Professor Muhamad Yunus.

    Resolved by the House of Representatives (the Senate concurring),

SECTION 1. USE OF ROTUNDA FOR CEREMONY TO AWARD CONGRESSIONAL GOLD 
              MEDAL TO PROFESSOR MUHAMAD YUNUS.

    The rotunda of the Capitol is authorized to be used on April 17, 
2013, for a ceremony to award the Congressional Gold Medal to Professor 
Muhamad Yunus in recognition of his contributions to the fight against 
global poverty. Physical preparations for the ceremony shall be carried 
out in accordance with such conditions as the Architect of the Capitol 
may prescribe.
