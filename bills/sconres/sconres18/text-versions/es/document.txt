S. CON. RES. 18

                         CONCURRENT RESOLUTION

    Resolved by the Senate (the House of Representatives concurring), 
That the Architect of the Capitol is authorized and directed to 
transfer the catafalque which is situated in the Exhibition Hall of the 
Capitol Visitor Center to the Senate Chamber so that such catafalque 
may be used in connection with services to be conducted there for the 
Honorable Frank R. Lautenberg, late a Senator from the State of New 
Jersey.

            Passed the Senate June 4, 2013.

            Attest:

                                                             Secretary.
