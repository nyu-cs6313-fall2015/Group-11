                                 A BILL


 
 To amend title 38, United States Code, to extend the authority of the 
 Secretary of Veterans Affairs to carry out the Department of Veterans 
            Affairs Health Professional Scholarship Program.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Healthcare for our Heroes Act''.

SEC. 2. EXTENSION OF DEPARTMENT OF VETERANS AFFAIRS HEALTH PROFESSIONAL 
              SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM.

    Section 7619 of title 38, United States Code, is amended by 
striking ``December 31, 2014'' and inserting ``December 31, 2016''.
