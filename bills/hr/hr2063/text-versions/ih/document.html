                                 A BILL


 
   To amend title 38, United States Code, to improve the health care 
provided to veterans of World War II at facilities of the Department of 
                           Veterans Affairs.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PROVISION OF HEALTH CARE TO VETERANS OF WORLD WAR II.

    Section 1705(a)(6) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by 
striking the period at the end and inserting the following: ``and 
veterans not covered by paragraphs (1) through (5) who served during 
World War II.''.
