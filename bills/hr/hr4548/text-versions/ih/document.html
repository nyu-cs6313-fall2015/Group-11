                                 A BILL


 
    To direct the Secretary of Labor to include programs that teach 
   technology literacy in any job training program for ex-offenders 
          offered under the Workforce Investment Act of 1998.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Re-Integration of Ex-Offenders 
Through Technology Act''.

SEC. 2. INCLUSION OF TECHNOLOGY LITERACY IN EX-OFFENDER JOB TRAINING 
              PROGRAMS.

    The Secretary of Labor shall include programs that teach technology 
literacy in any job training program for ex-offenders offered under the 
Workforce Investment Act of 1998 (29 U.S.C. 2801 et seq.).
