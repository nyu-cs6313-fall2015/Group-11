                                 A BILL


 
To prohibit the National Endowment for the Humanities to provide funds 
    to carry out the Popular Romance Project or any similar project 
                      relating to love or romance.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PROHIBITION.

    The National Endowment for the Humanities may not provide funds to 
carry out the Popular Romance Project or any similar project relating 
to love or romance.
