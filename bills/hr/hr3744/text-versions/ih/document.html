                                 A BILL


 
 To provide for the compensation of Federal employees furloughed as a 
                        result of sequestration.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Federal Employee Pay Restoration 
Act''.

SEC. 2. COMPENSATION FOR FEDERAL EMPLOYEES FURLOUGHED AS A RESULT OF 
              SEQUESTRATION.

    Federal employees furloughed as a result of reductions in budget 
authority required pursuant to the Presidential sequestration order 
dated March 1, 2013, shall be compensated at their standard rate of 
compensation for the period of such furlough.
