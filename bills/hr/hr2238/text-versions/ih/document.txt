                                 A BILL


 
To amend the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985 
          to exempt the Crime Victims Fund from sequestration.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EXEMPTION FROM SEQUESTRATION.

    Subsection (g)(1)(A) of section 255 of the Balanced Budget and 
Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985 is amended by inserting after the 
item relating to Continuing Fund, Southwestern Power Administration 
(the second place it appears) the following new item:
                    ``Crime Victims Fund (15-5041-0-2-754).''.
