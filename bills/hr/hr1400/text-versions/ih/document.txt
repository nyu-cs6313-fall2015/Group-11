                                 A BILL


 
  To amend title 38, United States Code, to clarify that children of 
  certain veterans are eligible for the Marine Gunnery Sergeant John 
                         David Fry scholarship.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. CLARIFICATION OF MARINE GUNNERY SERGEANT JOHN DAVID FRY 
              SCHOLARSHIP.

    (a) In General.--Section 3311(b)(9) of title 38, United States 
Code, is amended by striking ``in line of duty'' and all that follows 
through the period and inserting ``as a result of a service-connected 
disability.''.
    (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by subsection (a) shall 
take effect as if included in the enactment of section 1002 of the 
Supplemental Appropriations Act, 2009 (Public Law 111-32; 123 Stat. 
1889).
