                                 A BILL


 
   To extend the temporary suspension of duty on Disperse Blue 79:1.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DISPERSE BLUE 79:1.

    (a) In General.--Heading 9902.03.46 of the Harmonized Tariff 
Schedule of the United States (relating to Disperse Blue 79:1) is 
amended by striking the date in the effective period column and 
inserting ``12/31/2016''.
    (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by subsection (a) applies 
to goods entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or 
after the 15th day after the date of the enactment of this Act.
