                                 A BILL


 
 To prohibit the obligation or expenditure of funds made available to 
    any Federal department or agency for any fiscal year to provide 
  military assistance to any of the armed combatants in Syria absent 
          express prior statutory authorization from Congress.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PROHIBITION ON OBLIGATION AND EXPENDITURE OF FUNDS TO 
              PROVIDE MILITARY ASSISTANCE TO ANY OF THE ARMED 
              COMBATANTS IN SYRIA.

    None of the funds made available to any Federal department or 
agency for any fiscal year may be obligated or expended to provide 
military assistance to any of the armed combatants in Syria absent 
express prior statutory authorization from Congress.
