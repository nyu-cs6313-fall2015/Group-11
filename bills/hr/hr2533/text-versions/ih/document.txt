                                 A BILL


 
  To impose a moratorium on conferences held by the Internal Revenue 
                                Service.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Stop Playing on Citizen's Cash 
Act''.

SEC. 2. MORATORIUM ON IRS CONFERENCES.

    The Internal Revenue Service shall not hold any conference until--
            (1) the Treasury Inspector General for Tax Administration 
        submits a report to Congress--
                    (A) certifying that the Internal Revenue Service 
                has implemented all of the recommendations set out in 
                such Inspector General's report titled ``Review of the 
                August 2010 Small Business/Self-Employed Division's 
                Conference in Anaheim, California'', and
                    (B) describing such implementation, and
            (2) the Internal Revenue Service is authorized by law 
        (enacted after the date of the enactment of this Act) to resume 
        holding conferences.
