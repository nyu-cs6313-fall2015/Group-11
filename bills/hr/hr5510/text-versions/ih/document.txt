                                 A BILL


 
To amend the Higher Education Act of 1965 to provide for more effective 
                 online education verification metrics.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Online Education Verification Act''.

SEC. 2. REQUIREMENT FOR SECURE LOGIN AND PASSCODE.

    Section 496(a)(4)(B)(ii) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 (20 
U.S.C. 1099b(a)(4)(B)(ii)) is amended by inserting after ``processes'' 
the following: ``, in addition to requiring a secure login and passcode 
for coursework on the Internet,''.
