                                 A BILL


 
To remove from the John H. Chafee Coastal Barrier Resources System the 
           areas comprising Bay County Unit P-31P in Florida.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REMOVAL FROM JOHN H. CHAFEE COASTAL BARRIER RESOURCES SYSTEM 
              OF AREAS COMPRISING BAY COUNTY UNIT P-31P, FLORIDA.

    The areas comprising John H. Chafee Coastal Barrier Resources 
System Bay County Unit P-31P in Florida immediately before the 
enactment of this Act are not part of such system, and maps relating to 
such unit are hereby removed from the maps referred to in section 4(a) 
of the Coastal Barrier Resources Act (16 U.S.C. 3503(a)).
