                                 A BILL


 
 To extend the authorization period for certain uses of funds from the 
                  San Gabriel Basin Restoration Fund.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SAN GABRIEL BASIN RESTORATION.

    (a) Extension.--Section 110(a)(3)(A)(ii) of division B of the 
Miscellaneous Appropriations Act, 2001 (as enacted into law by section 
1(a)(4) of Public Law 106-554) is amended by striking ``10'' and 
inserting ``15''.
    (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by subsection (a) shall 
apply to any project constructed under Section 110 of such Act before, 
on, or after the date of enactment of this Act.
