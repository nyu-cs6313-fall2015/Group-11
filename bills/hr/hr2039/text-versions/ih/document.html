                                 A BILL


 
 To amend title 38, United States Code, to authorize the Secretary of 
Veterans Affairs to furnish headstones and markers for certain deceased 
       veterans buried in veterans' cemeteries of Indian tribes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. HEADSTONES AND MARKERS FOR PLACEMENT IN TRIBAL VETERANS' 
              CEMETERIES.

    Section 2306 of title 38, United States Code, is amended--
            (1) in subsection (a)(4), by inserting ``or in a veterans' 
        cemetery on land owned by or held in trust for a tribal 
        organization (as defined in section 3765(4) of this title)'' 
        after ``State''; and
            (2) in subsection (b)(1), by inserting ``a veterans' 
        cemetery on land owned by or held in trust for a tribal 
        organization,'' after ``a veterans' cemetery owned by a 
        State,''.
