                                 A BILL


 
To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to exclude from gross income 
 discharges of Federal student loans as a result of veterans' service-
        connected total disability that is permanent in nature.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Veteran Tax Parity Act''.

SEC. 2. EXCLUSION FROM GROSS INCOME FOR DISCHARGE OF FEDERAL STUDENT 
              LOANS OF VETERANS AS A RESULT OF PERMANENT SERVICE-
              CONNECTED TOTAL DISABILITY.

    (a) In General.--Paragraph (1) of section 108(f) of the Internal 
Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by inserting ``or if such discharge 
resulted from an individual having a service-connected total disability 
that is permanent in nature as determined by the Secretary of Veterans 
Affairs'' before the period at the end.
