                                 A BILL


 
             To prohibit funding to the Institute of Peace.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PROHIBITION ON FUNDING THE INSTITUTE OF PEACE.

    No funds available to the Department of State or any other 
department or agency may be used to provide contributions directly or 
indirectly to the Institute of Peace.
