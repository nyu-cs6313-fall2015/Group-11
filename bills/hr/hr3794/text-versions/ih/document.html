                                 A BILL


 
To repeal the annual adjustment of retired pay and retainer pay amounts 
  for retired members of the Armed Forces under age 62, and for other 
                               purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REPEAL OF ANNUAL ADJUSTMENT OF RETIRED PAY AND RETAINER PAY 
              AMOUNTS FOR RETIRED MEMBERS OF THE ARMED FORCES UNDER AGE 
              62.

    (a) Repeal.--Section 403 of the Bipartisan Budget Act of 2013 is 
hereby repealed.
    (b) Offset.--To offset the costs to be incurred by the repeal made 
by subsection (a), the unobligated balances of the Department of 
Defense shall be reduced as necessary to generate savings in the total 
amount of $6,200,000,000.
