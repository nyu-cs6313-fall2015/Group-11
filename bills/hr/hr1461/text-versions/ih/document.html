                                 A BILL


 
 To repeal the renewable fuel program of the Environmental Protection 
                                Agency.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Renewable Fuel Standard Elimination 
Act''.

SEC. 2. REPEAL OF RENEWABLE FUEL PROGRAM.

    Section 211(o) of the Clean Air Act (42 U.S.C. 7545(o)) is 
repealed.
