                                 A BILL


 
To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to make the work opportunity 
                         tax credit permanent.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``American Job Opportunity Act''.

SEC. 2. WORK OPPORTUNITY TAX CREDIT MADE PERMANENT.

    (a) In General.--Section 51(c) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 
is amended by striking paragraph (4).
    (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by subsection (a) shall 
apply to wages paid after December 31, 2013.
