                                 A BILL


 
  To amend the National Institute of Standards and Technology Act to 
   remove the National Security Agency from the list of the entities 
 consulted during the development of information systems standards and 
                              guidelines.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. INFORMATION SYSTEMS STANDARDS CONSULTATION.

    Section 20(c)(1) of the National Institute of Standards and 
Technology Act (15 U.S.C. 278g-3(c)(1)) is amended by striking ``the 
National Security Agency,''.
