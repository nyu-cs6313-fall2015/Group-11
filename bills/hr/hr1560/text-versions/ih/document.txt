                                 A BILL


 
 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to authorize the Internal 
  Revenue Service to permit truncated social security numbers on wage 
                    reporting provided to employees.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``SAFE ID Act of 2013''.

SEC. 2. TRUNCATED SOCIAL SECURITY NUMBERS PERMITTED ON WAGE REPORTING 
              TO EMPLOYEES.

    (a) In General.--Paragraph (2) of section 6051(a) of the Internal 
Revenue Code for 1986 is amended by striking ``social security number'' 
and inserting ``taxpayer identification number''.
    (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by this section shall apply 
to statements furnished after the date of the enactment of this Act.
