                                 A BILL


 
 To amend the East Bench Irrigation District Water Contract Extension 
Act to permit the Secretary of the Interior to extend the contract for 
                        certain water services.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EAST BENCH IRRIGATION DISTRICT CONTRACT EXTENSION.

    Section 2(1) of the East Bench Irrigation District Water Contract 
Extension Act (Public Law 112-139; 126 Stat. 390) is amended by 
striking ``4 years'' and inserting ``10 years''.
