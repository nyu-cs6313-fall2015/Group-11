                                 A BILL


 
To ensure that the Metropolitan Washington Airports Authority complies 
             with certain Federal regulations and statutes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. APPLICATION OF FEDERAL REGULATIONS AND STATUTES.

    (a) Federal Acquisition Regulation.--The Metropolitan Washington 
Airports Authority shall comply with the Federal Acquisition 
Regulation.
    (b) Employment of Relatives; Restrictions.--Section 3110(a)(1) of 
title 5, United States Code, is amended--
            (1) in subparagraph (C) by striking ``and'';
            (2) by redesignating subparagraph (D) as subparagraph (E); 
        and
            (3) by inserting after subparagraph (C) the following:
                    ``(D) the Metropolitan Washington Airports 
                Authority; and''.
