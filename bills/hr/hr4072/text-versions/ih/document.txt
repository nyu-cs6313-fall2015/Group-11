                                 A BILL


 
To prohibit the use of funds appropriated to any Federal department or 
 agency to provide technical assistance to nongovernment entities for 
    the production of motion pictures, television shows, and other 
                         audiovisual programs.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PROHIBITION ON USE OF FEDERAL FUNDS TO PROVIDE TECHNICAL 
              ASSISTANCE FOR MOTION PICTURES, TELEVISION SHOWS, AND 
              OTHER AUDIOVISUAL PROGRAMS.

    No Federal department or agency may obligate or expend funds 
appropriated to the department or agency for a fiscal year to provide 
technical assistance to any nongovernment entity for the production of 
any motion picture, television show, documentary, music videos, 
commercial advertisement, CD-ROM game, or other audiovisual program.
