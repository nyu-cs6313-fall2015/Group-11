                                 A BILL


 
  To prohibit the negotiation of trade agreements that include waivers 
                      of the ``Buy American Act''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PROHIBITION ON NEGOTIATING TRADE AGREEMENTS CONTAINING 
              WAIVERS OF THE ``BUY AMERICAN ACT''.

    No funds appropriated for fiscal year 2015 or any year thereafter 
may be used to negotiate an agreement that includes a waiver of the 
``Buy American Act'' (41 U.S.C. 8301 et seq.).
