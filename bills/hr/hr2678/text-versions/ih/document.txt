                                 A BILL


 
 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located 
at 10360 Southwest 186th Street in Miami, Florida, as the ``Larcenia J. 
                    Bullard Post Office Building''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. LARCENIA J. BULLARD POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 10360 Southwest 186th Street in Miami, Florida, shall be 
known and designated as the ``Larcenia J. Bullard Post Office 
Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Larcenia 
J. Bullard Post Office Building''.
