                                 A BILL


 
   To prohibit certain nutrition rules with respect to foods sold at 
                        schools as a fundraiser.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Bringing Awareness and Knowledge to 
Exempt Schools Against Legislative Encroachment Act''.

SEC. 2. PROHIBITION OF CERTAIN RULES.

    The Secretary of Agriculture shall not implement, administer, or 
enforce the rule entitled ``National School Lunch Program and School 
Breakfast Program: Nutrition Standards for All Foods Sold in School as 
Required by the Healthy, Hunger-Free Kids Act of 2010'' published by 
the Department of Agriculture in the Federal Register on June 28, 2013 
(78 Fed. Reg. 39068 et seq.) with respect to food sold as a fundraiser 
that takes place on the grounds of a school.
