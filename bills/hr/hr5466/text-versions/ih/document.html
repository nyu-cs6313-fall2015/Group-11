                                 A BILL


 
 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located 
at 4500 Sunny Isle Shopping Center in Christiansted, St. Croix, United 
 States Virgin Islands, as the ``Florence Louise Thomas Post Office''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. FLORENCE LOUISE THOMAS POST OFFICE.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 4500 Sunny Isle Shopping Center in Christiansted, St. Croix, 
United States Virgin Islands, shall be known and designated as the 
``Florence Louise Thomas Post Office''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Florence 
Louise Thomas Post Office''.
