                                 A BILL


 
 To repeal the reduction in the annual percentage increases of retired 
 pay and retainer pay amounts for retired members of the Armed Forces 
                             under age 62.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REPEAL OF REDUCED ANNUAL PERCENTAGE INCREASES OF RETIRED PAY 
              AND RETAINER PAY AMOUNTS FOR RETIRED MEMBERS OF THE ARMED 
              FORCES UNDER AGE 62.

    Section 403 of the Bipartisan Budget Act of 2013 is hereby 
repealed.
