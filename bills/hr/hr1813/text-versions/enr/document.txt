                                 An Act


 
To redesignate the facility of the United States Postal Service located 
  at 162 Northeast Avenue in Tallmadge, Ohio, as the ``Lance Corporal 
          Daniel Nathan Deyarmin, Jr., Post Office Building''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. LANCE CORPORAL DANIEL NATHAN DEYARMIN, JR., POST OFFICE 
BUILDING.
    (a) Redesignation.--The facility of the United States Postal 
Service located at 162 Northeast Avenue in Tallmadge, Ohio, shall be 
known and designated as the ``Lance Corporal Daniel Nathan Deyarmin, 
Jr., Post Office Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Lance 
Corporal Daniel Nathan Deyarmin, Jr., Post Office Building''.
