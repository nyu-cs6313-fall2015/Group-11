                                 AN ACT


 
 Designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
 25 South Oak Street in London, Ohio, as the ``London Fallen Veterans 
                        Memorial Post Office''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. LONDON FALLEN VETERANS MEMORIAL POST OFFICE.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 25 South Oak Street in London, Ohio, shall be known and 
designated as the ``London Fallen Veterans Memorial Post Office''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``London 
Fallen Veterans Memorial Post Office''.

            Passed the House of Representatives June 17, 2014.
