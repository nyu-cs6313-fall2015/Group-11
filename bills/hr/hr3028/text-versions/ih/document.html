                                 A BILL


 
To amend the hold harmless provision for career and technical education 
                      assistance grants to States.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Career and Technical Education 
Equity Act''.

SEC. 2. HOLD HARMLESS.

    Section 111(a)(5)(A) of the Carl D. Perkins Career and Technical 
Education Act of 2006 (20 U.S.C. 2321(a)(5)(A)) is amended to read as 
follows:
                    ``(A) In general.--No State shall receive an 
                allotment under this section for a fiscal year that is 
                less than 90 percent of the allotment the State 
                received under this section for the preceding fiscal 
                year.''.
