                                 A BILL


 
     To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to allow Medicare 
 beneficiaries participating in a Medicare Advantage MSA to contribute 
                     their own money to their MSA.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. MEDICARE BENEFICIARIES PARTICIPATING IN MEDICARE ADVANTAGE 
              MSA MAY CONTRIBUTE THEIR OWN MONEY TO THEIR MSA.

    (a) In General.--Subsection (b) of section 138 of the Internal 
Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking paragraph (2) and by 
redesignating paragraphs (3) and (4) as paragraphs (2) and (3), 
respectively.
    (b) Effective Date.--The amendments made by this section shall 
apply to taxable years beginning after the date of the enactment of 
this Act.
