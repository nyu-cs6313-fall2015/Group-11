                                 A BILL


 
To authorize the construction of the expansion of San Luis Reservoir in 
                              California.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``San Luis Reservoir Expansion Act of 
2014''.

SEC. 2. SAN LUIS RESERVOIR EXPANSION.

    Section 103(f) of Title I of Public Law 108-361 (the Calfed Bay-
Delta Authorization Act, 118 Stat. 1681) is amended by--
     (a) Striking subparagraph (1) and inserting the following:
            ``(1) Conveyance and expansion.--The amounts authorized to 
        be appropriated under section 109 may be expended for the 
        following.''
    (b) Inserting ``including the increase of storage capacity at San 
Luis Reservoir,'' after ``improvement project,''.
