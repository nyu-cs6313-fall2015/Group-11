                               RESOLUTION


 
   To amend the Standing Rules of the Senate to modify the provision 
           relating to timing for filing of cloture motions.

    Resolved,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This resolution may be cited as the ``Stop Cloture Abuse 
Resolution''.

SEC. 2. TIME PRE-CLOTURE.

    Paragraph 2 of rule XXII of the Standing Rules of the Senate is 
amended in the first undesignated subparagraph--
            (1) by inserting ``after the end of the 24-hour period 
        beginning at the time the Senate proceeds to consideration of a 
        measure, motion, or other matter'' after ``at any time''; and
            (2) by striking ``any measure'' and inserting ``the 
        measure''.
