                                 A BILL


 
          To assist survivors of stroke in returning to work.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Return to Work Act of 2013''.

SEC. 2. ASSISTING STROKE SURVIVORS IN RETURNING TO WORK.

    The Secretary of Labor, acting through the Job Accommodation 
Network, may promote awareness and assistance among employers to enable 
survivors of stroke to return to work.
