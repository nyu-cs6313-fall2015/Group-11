                                 A BILL


 
 To prohibit the use of funds for United States participation in joint 
  military exercises with Egypt if the Government of Egypt abrogates, 
   terminates, or withdraws from the 1979 Egypt-Israel peace treaty.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Conditioning United States-Egypt 
Military Exercises Act of 2013''.

SEC. 2. CONTINGENT LIMITATION ON AVAILABILITY OF FUNDS FOR UNITED 
              STATES PARTICIPATION IN JOINT MILITARY EXERCISES WITH 
              EGYPT.

    (a) Limitation.--No amounts appropriated or otherwise made 
available for the United States Government may be used for United 
States participation in joint military exercises with Egypt if the 
Government of Egypt abrogates, terminates, or withdraws from the 1979 
Egypt-Israel peace treaty signed at Washington, DC, on March 26, 1979.
    (b) Waiver.--The President may waive the limitation in subsection 
(a) if the President certifies to Congress in writing that the waiver 
is in the national security interests of the United States.
