                                 A BILL


 
  To amend Public Law 110-299 to extend the time period during which 
   permits are not required for certain discharges incidental to the 
                      normal operation of vessels.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DISCHARGES INCIDENTAL TO NORMAL OPERATION OF VESSELS.

    Section 2(a) of Public Law 110-299 (33 U.S.C. 1342 note) is amended 
by striking ``December 18, 2014'' and inserting ``December 18, 2015''.
