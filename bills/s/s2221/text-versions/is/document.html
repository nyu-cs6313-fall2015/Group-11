                                 A BILL


 
 To extend the authorization for the Automobile National Heritage Area 
                              in Michigan.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``MotorCities National Heritage Area 
Extension Act''.

SEC. 2. AUTOMOBILE NATIONAL HERITAGE AREA AUTHORIZATION EXTENDED.

    Section 109 of the Automobile National Heritage Area Act (16 U.S.C. 
461 note; Public Law 105-355) is amended by striking ``September 30, 
2014'' and inserting ``September 30, 2030''.
