                                 A BILL


 
                  For the relief of Dr. Shakil Afridi.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. NATURALIZATION FOR DR. SHAKIL AFRIDI.

    Notwithstanding title III of the Immigration and Nationality Act, 
or any other provision of law relating to naturalization, Dr. Shakil 
Afridi is deemed to be a naturalized citizen of the United States as of 
the date of the enactment of this Act.
