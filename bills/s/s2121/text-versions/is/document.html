                                 A BILL


 
             To repeal title II of the REAL ID Act of 2005.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Repeal REAL ID Act''.

SEC. 2. REPEAL OF TITLE II OF THE REAL ID ACT OF 2005.

    Title II of the REAL ID Act of 2005 (division B of Public Law 109-
13; 49 U.S.C. 30301 note) is repealed.
