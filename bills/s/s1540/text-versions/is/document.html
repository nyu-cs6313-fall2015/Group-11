                                 A BILL


 
To amend title 38, United States Code, to include contracts and grants 
 for residential care for veterans in the exception to the requirement 
 that the Federal Government recover a portion of the value of certain 
                               projects.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. INCLUSION OF CONTRACTS AND GRANTS FOR RESIDENTIAL CARE FOR 
              VETERANS IN EXCEPTION TO CERTAIN RECAPTURE REQUIREMENTS.

    Section 8136(b) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by 
inserting ``, or the provision by the Secretary of a contract or grant 
for residential care for veterans,'' after ``outpatient clinic''.
